# Inputs not redistributed here

The following inputs are registered in ../SOURCES.md (URL, access date,
SHA-256) but are not committed, because their publishers' terms do not
clearly permit redistribution. Fetch each from its publisher and verify
the hash; place SARB and KNBS files under the paths below, and the
UNU-WIDER and DataFirst files under ../external/ (git-ignored).

- sarb/ — three SARB Quarterly Bulletin bulk zips (December 2022): S-004
- kenya/knbs/ — KNBS Economic Surveys and rebasing reports: S-009, S-010
- ../external/ — UNU-WIDER 2019 SAM + technical note (S-003; audit
  benchmark) and DataFirst microdata (S-005, S-006; access-controlled,
  never redistributed)

Scripts state their inputs in their docstrings; everything else in the
pipeline runs from the committed files alone.
